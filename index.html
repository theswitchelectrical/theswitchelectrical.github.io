LED Lighting

The Switch also offers full LED conversion and installation services. There are many benefits to switching to LED lighting from conventional light sources:


  • Longer Lasting. LED lights last up to 10 times longer than halogen lamps. In some cases, LED lights can last up to 80,000 hours!

  • More Energy Efficient. LED lights are extremely energy efficient, using only 15% of the energy a standard halogen lamp uses.

  • Safer. LED lights operate at lower voltages to halogen lights, so are cool to touch. They're much safer to handle during installation and maintenance.

  • Instantly Bright. LED lights start at full brightness; they don't need time to warm up, light a conventional halogen lamp.

  • Ecologically Friendly. LED lights are an eco-friendly form of lighting as they do not contain harmful gasses and do not emit UV rays.

  • Environmentally Friendly. A 15w LED light produces 68% less CO2 emissions than 40w incandescent bulb!
